Festivals

Chinese New Year

In the festivities of Chinese New Year, the 24-section tambourine takes center stage as a symbol of joy, prosperity, and unity. Its rhythmic beats, resonating with the spirit of new beginnings, accompany dragon and lion dances, filling the air with an electrifying energy that ushers in good luck for the year ahead.

Independence Day

A time of national pride and patriotism, also witnesses the exuberant presence of the 24-section tambourine. Its spirited cadence amplifies the fervor of parades and gatherings, underscoring the shared history and triumphs of a nation.

Mid-Autumn Festival

Accompanying graceful lantern processions and moonlit gatherings, its gentle tones evoke a sense of nostalgia and togetherness.
