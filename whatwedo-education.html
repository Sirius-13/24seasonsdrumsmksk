Coaching

Our coaching offer a dynamic and engaging learning journey. Discover the intricate rhythms, expressive melodies, and cultural nuances that define the 24-section tambourine's allure. Whether you're interested in performing at special occasions, joining cultural ensembles, or simply indulging in a fulfilling musical pursuit, our tambourine coaching provide the guidance and resources you need to embark on a rewarding musical adventure.
