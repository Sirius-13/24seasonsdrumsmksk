Founder Consultant Coach
Lim Ewe Guan
Consultant
Mr. Lim Ewe Guan, a distinguished educator, left an indelible mark on SMK Seri Kembangan. Serving as a Consultant Teacher for the 24 Season Drum, he also led the English Department, contributing significantly to academic excellence and cultural enrichment.

Under his guidance, the 24 Season Drum team achieved remarkable success, garnering recognition within and beyond the school.

Mr. Lim's visionary leadership extended beyond the classroom, captivating audiences with mesmerizing drumming performances at various external venues.

His retirement in 2020 marked the end of an era characterized by unwavering dedication, innovative teaching, and a profound impact on both students' academic pursuits and artistic talents.
