24 Seasons Drum

1989

  • Founded by Malaysian Chinese Chen Hui Chong and Chen Zai Fan.

2009

  • 24 Seasons Drum become national heritage project in Malaysia.

The performance form of the 24 Seasons Drums uses 24 single-skin large drums to represent the 24 solar terms.
